Idea , innovation , vision and challenges in New Bangladesh.

Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh 2.0 is a movement that aims to improve the country through innovation in infrastructure, governance, and social reform

Sustainability

The Bangladesh 2.0 agenda envisions a future where economic growth is not achieved at the expense of the environment

Infrastructure

Bangladesh 2.0 includes investments in digital infrastructure, transportation, and sustainable infrastructure

Empowerment

The government has taken steps to empower women and children, including improving access to education and health services
