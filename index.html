Pillars of "Bangladesh 2.0"

Innovation

Bangladesh excels in ICT, renewable energy, and agriculture, fostering startups and digitalization to drive economic growth and sustainable development.

Sustainability

Bangladesh promotes sustainability through green energy, eco-friendly farming, waste management, and climate resilience to ensure long-term environmental and economic stability

Infrastructure

Bangladesh is rapidly developing infrastructure with modern highways, bridges, metro rail, and digital connectivity to support economic growth and urbanization.

Empowerment

Bangladesh fosters empowerment through education, women's entrepreneurship, digital inclusion, and skill development, enabling individuals to contribute to national progress.

The Vision of Future

The vision for the future of Bangladesh 2.0 focuses on digital transformation, sustainable development, economic diversification, and social empowerment, aiming for a prosperous, technology-driven, and inclusive nation.

Digital Transformation

Bangladesh is embracing digital transformation through smart governance, e-commerce, fintech, and widespread internet connectivity to accelerate economic growth and social inclusion.

Green Energy

Bangladesh is advancing green energy through solar power, wind energy, and eco-friendly initiatives to ensure a sustainable and low-carbon future.

Modern Infrastructure

Bangladesh is developing modern infrastructure with smart cities, efficient transportation networks, and advanced digital connectivity to support economic growth and urbanization.
